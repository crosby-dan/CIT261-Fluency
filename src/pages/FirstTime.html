Congratulations and thank you for becoming a pet adopter!

    Instructions:

  1. Adopt one or more pets by typing in a name and clicking the image of the desired pet.
  2. When pets get hungry, click the food bowl below their name.
  3. Pets are messy, don't forget to click on your backyard from time to time and clean up!

    Tips:

  • If a pet is misbehaving, you can place a single pet in timeout. Don't forget to remove him/her later!
  • If you need a break, you can check one or more pets into the pet hotel for an indefinite period of time.
  • Note: All changes are saved automatically into your browser local storage.
